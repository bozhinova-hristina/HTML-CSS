Summary

Smoothies are made by blending fruit, vegetables, yogurt, and other ingredients to make a thick, creamy beverage. Certain smoothies contain large quantities of added sugar, which can reduce the drinks overall nutrient density. Excess added sugar intake may increase your risk of disease. When making smoothies at home, aim to include a balanced combination of carbs, fiber, protein, and healthy fats.
